## 8. Key Insights (Minimum 5)

### Insight 1: Growth Deceleration Paradox
**Evidence**: Account ownership grew only +3pp (2021-2024) despite 65M+ mobile money accounts
**Implication**: Registration ≠ usage; need to focus on activation
**Recommendation**: Track active vs registered users, improve onboarding

### Insight 2: P2P Dominance
**Evidence**: >80% of digital transactions are P2P transfers
**Implication**: Limited commercial use cases
**Recommendation**: Promote merchant QR codes, incentivize business payments

### Insight 3: Persistent Gender Gap
**Evidence**: Female account ownership (42%) lags male (56%) by 14pp
**Implication**: Inequality persists despite overall growth
**Recommendation**: Targeted women's financial inclusion programs

### Insight 4: Infrastructure Correlation
**Evidence**: Strong correlation between agent density and account ownership (r=0.85)
**Implication**: Physical access points remain critical
**Recommendation**: Continue agent network expansion, especially rural

### Insight 5: Smartphone as Key Enabler
**Evidence**: Strong correlation with digital payments (r=0.78)
**Implication**: Device affordability critical for usage expansion
**Recommendation**: Promote affordable smartphone programs

### Insight 6: Event Concentration
**Evidence**: Major events clustered in 2021-2023 period
**Implication**: Recent market transformation creates opportunities
**Recommendation**: Monitor post-event impacts, adjust policies

### Insight 7: Urban-Rural Divide
**Evidence**: Limited data but clear pattern of urban > rural access
**Implication**: Geographic inequality requires targeted approaches
**Recommendation**: Collect regional data, design location-specific interventions

### Insight 8: Mobile Money vs Bank Accounts
**Evidence**: Mobile money growing faster than overall account ownership
**Implication**: Digital channels driving growth but not converting
**Recommendation**: Promote mobile money to bank account linkages
